## What is fortISSimO?

This Game Boy music driver can be used as a drop-in replacement of [SuperDisk's GB sound driver][hUGEDriver].
It has, however, [a few differences](./faq.md#what-do-i-stand-to-gain-from-using-fo-over-hd).
